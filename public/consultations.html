Process

How Digital Consultations Work

From choosing a doctor to completing your consultation — in 5 simple steps

1

Choose Doctor

Browse our list of certified specialists, read their profiles, and select the doctor that best matches your healthcare needs.

2

Request Consultation

Submit a consultation request with your symptoms and preferred date. Your request will be reviewed by the doctor.

3

Doctor Review

The doctor reviews your request and symptoms. Once accepted, you'll be notified and can proceed to scheduling.

4

Schedule Session

Pick a convenient date and time for your video consultation. You'll receive the consultation link before the session.

5

Consultation Completed

Connect with your doctor via secure video call. Get diagnosis, prescription, and follow-up recommendations.
