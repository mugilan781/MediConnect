Patient FAQs

Everything you need to know about appointments, consultations, lab tests, and medical reports.

Log in to your MediConnect account, go to the Patient Dashboard and click the Appointments tab. Search for your preferred doctor or specialty, select an available date and time slot, and confirm your booking. You'll receive an instant confirmation via email and SMS.
Yes, you can reschedule or cancel appointments up to 2 hours before the scheduled time from your Patient Dashboard. Cancellations made more than 24 hours in advance receive a full refund. Same-day cancellations may incur a nominal processing fee.
After booking an online consultation, you'll receive a secure video link before the appointment. At the scheduled time, click the link from your dashboard to join the video call with your doctor. Consultations are HIPAA-compliant and end-to-end encrypted for your privacy.
Navigate to the Lab Tests section from your dashboard or the main menu. Browse available tests by category, select the tests you need, choose between lab visit or home sample collection, and complete the booking. Test results are automatically uploaded to your account.
All your medical reports are stored securely in your MediConnect account. Go to the Reports section from your dashboard to view, download, or share your reports. You can organize them by date, category, or doctor for easy access anytime.
Yes, home sample collection is available for selected lab tests. When booking a lab test, choose the "Home Collection" option, select your preferred time slot, and our trained phlebotomist will visit your address. Results are delivered digitally within the test's turnaround time.

Doctor FAQs

For healthcare professionals managing their practice on MediConnect.

From your Doctor Dashboard, use the Schedule section to set your availability, block time slots, and manage recurring appointments. You can set different hours for different days of the week and update your schedule in real time. Patients will only see available slots.
The Patient History section in your dashboard gives you a complete view of each patient's medical records, including past consultations, lab results, prescriptions, and uploaded reports. All data is presented chronologically for easy review during consultations.
Yes, MediConnect supports secure video consultations directly from your dashboard. You can start a consultation with a single click at the scheduled time. The platform handles video routing, recording (with consent), and automatically logs the consultation to the patient's history.
After a consultation, use the e-Prescription feature to generate digital prescriptions. Select medications, specify dosage and duration, and sign digitally. Prescriptions are sent to the patient's account and can be printed or shared with pharmacies electronically.
Use the Follow-Up feature in your dashboard to schedule automatic reminders for patients who need follow-up consultations. You can set the follow-up interval, send reminders, and track which patients have completed their follow-up appointments.

Admin FAQs

Platform administration, content management, and operational controls.

From the Admin Dashboard, navigate to the CMS section to manage FAQs, banners, and page content. You can add, edit, reorder, or deactivate FAQ items. Changes are reflected immediately on the public-facing pages. The CMS supports rich text formatting for detailed answers.
Use the Notifications section in the Admin Dashboard to create and send targeted notifications. You can filter by user role (patient, doctor), send SMS, email, or in-app notifications, and schedule them for future delivery. Notification history is logged for audit purposes.
The Analytics Dashboard provides real-time metrics on appointments, user registrations, revenue, consultation completion rates, and platform usage trends. You can export reports in CSV or PDF format and set custom date ranges for detailed analysis.
From the User Management section, you can view, activate, deactivate, or assign roles to users. MediConnect supports three roles: Patient, Doctor, and Admin. Role-based access control ensures each user sees only the features relevant to their role.

Security & Privacy FAQs

How MediConnect protects your healthcare data and ensures compliance.

MediConnect uses end-to-end encryption (AES-256) for all data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. We implement role-based access control, multi-factor authentication, and regular security audits. Our infrastructure is hosted on SOC 2-compliant cloud providers.
Yes, MediConnect is fully HIPAA compliant. We implement administrative, physical, and technical safeguards required by HIPAA regulations, including access controls, audit trails, integrity controls, and secure transmission of electronic protected health information (ePHI).
Your medical records are only accessible to you and the healthcare providers you authorize. Doctors can only view records of their own patients. Administrators have limited access for platform operations. You can revoke or grant access at any time from your account settings.
All video consultations are end-to-end encrypted using WebRTC with DTLS-SRTP encryption. Video streams are never stored on our servers unless explicitly recorded with patient consent. Recorded consultations are encrypted and stored in secure, access-controlled storage.
Automated encrypted backups are performed every 6 hours across geographically redundant data centers. We maintain a 30-day backup retention policy with point-in-time recovery capability. Disaster recovery procedures are tested quarterly to ensure RPO and RTO compliance.

Account Management FAQs

Managing your profile, login, and account preferences.

Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage or navigate to the signup page. Fill in your name, email address, phone number, and create a secure password. Select your role (Patient, Doctor, or Admin) and complete the registration. You'll receive a verification email to activate your account.
On the login page, click "Forgot Password." Enter your registered email address and we'll send you a password reset link. Click the link and follow the instructions to set a new password. For security, reset links expire after 30 minutes.
Log in to your account and navigate to Profile Settings from the account dropdown menu. You can update your name, contact information, profile photo, and notification preferences. Changes are saved instantly and reflected across the platform.
To delete your account, go to Profile Settings and select the "Delete Account" option. You'll be asked to confirm your decision. Account deletion is irreversible and removes all personal data within 30 days per our data retention policy. Download any important records before deletion.
Yes, your MediConnect account can be accessed from multiple devices simultaneously — desktop, tablet, and mobile. All your data syncs in real time across devices. For security, you can view and manage active sessions from your account settings.

Billing & Payments FAQs

Payment methods, subscription management, and refund policies.

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), UPI, net banking, and popular digital wallets. Enterprise plans can opt for invoicing with net-30 payment terms. All payments are processed securely through PCI-compliant payment gateways.
All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial with full feature access. No credit card is required to start. You can cancel anytime during the trial period with no charges. After the trial, you'll be prompted to choose a plan and enter payment details to continue.
Yes, you can change your plan at any time from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing for the remainder of the billing cycle. Downgrades apply at the start of the next billing cycle. Your data is always preserved during plan changes.
Cancellations made more than 24 hours before the scheduled appointment receive a full refund. For cancellations within 24 hours, a nominal processing fee may apply. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days and credited to the original payment method.
You can cancel your subscription anytime from the Billing section in your account settings. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. We'll provide a data export option before cancellation so you don't lose any important records.
