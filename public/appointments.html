How Appointments Work

From finding a doctor to visit confirmation — in 7 simple steps

1

Search Doctor

Browse our directory by name, specialization, or department to find the right healthcare professional for your needs.

2

Choose Doctor

Review doctor profiles including qualifications, experience, consultation fees, and patient ratings to make an informed choice.

3

Select Date

Pick your preferred appointment date from the interactive calendar. Our system shows real-time availability for each day.

4

Select Slot

Choose from available time slots that fit your schedule. Each slot is dynamically generated based on the doctor's schedule.

5

Book Appointment

Confirm your booking with one click. You'll receive an instant confirmation via email and SMS.

6

Doctor Approval

The doctor reviews and confirms your appointment. You'll be notified immediately of any status changes.

7

Visit Confirmation

Your appointment is confirmed. Get ready for your visit with automated reminders and easy rescheduling options.
