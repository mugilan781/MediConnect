Safety First

Our Safety Standards

Your health and safety are our top priority at every step

Sterile Equipment

All equipment is single-use and sterilized. No reuse of needles or collection devices.

PPE Compliance

Our technicians wear full PPE including masks, gloves, and sanitized uniforms for every visit.

Certified Staff

All phlebotomists are certified, background-checked, and trained in infection control protocols.

Cold Chain Transport

Samples are transported in temperature-controlled containers to maintain integrity.

Biohazard Disposal

All biomedical waste is disposed of following strict regulatory guidelines and protocols.

Quality Audits

Regular quality audits and surprise inspections ensure consistent safety compliance.
